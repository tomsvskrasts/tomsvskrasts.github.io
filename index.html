Divergent Continuums

How did legal status change the face of freedom? Explore the difference between a Satellite State (Poland) and an Occupied Republic (Latvia).

The Reformist Path

Driven by the Solidarity trade union and the 1989 Round Table Talks. Poland sought to modernise an existing state.

"The shops had shortages, the queues were long... then came the Round Table. It was hope on the horizon."
— Polish Primary Witness

Economic Model: Shock Therapy (Balcerowicz Plan).

The Restorationist Path

Driven by the Singing Revolution. Latvia sought to restore a state stolen in 1940. A total institutional break.

"The shops were truly empty... everything was on coupons. But the younger generation lives in paradise now and they don't know it."
— Latvian Primary Witness

Economic Model: The Coupon Economy & Ruble Collapse.
